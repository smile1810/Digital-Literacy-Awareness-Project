**Stay Safe Online — Prevention Checklist**



**General Online Safety**

\* Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts

\* Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible

\* Do not click on suspicious links from unknown emails or messages

\* Avoid sharing personal information on public platforms

\* Always verify website URLs before entering login details

\* Keep your devices and apps updated regularly

\* Avoid using public Wi-Fi for sensitive transactions

\* Log out from shared or public computers after use

**UPI / Financial Safety**

\* Never approve unknown payment requests on UPI apps

\* Do not scan QR codes to receive money (QR codes are for sending money only)

\* Never share OTP, UPI PIN, or bank details with anyone

\* Verify the receiver's name before making any payment

\* Avoid installing unknown screen-sharing or remote access apps

\* Check transaction notifications immediately after payment

**Report Cybercrime**

\* National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
