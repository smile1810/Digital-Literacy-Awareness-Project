Social     Media    DO’S    And     DON’T’S 
                             Checklist

Do’s
* Think before posting—consider how it may affect your reputation
* Use privacy settings to control who sees your content
* Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation
* Be respectful and polite in comments and messages
* Maintain a professional tone, especially on platforms like LinkedIn



Dont’s
* Don’t share personal or sensitive information publicly
* Don’t engage in cyberbullying or offensive behavior
* Don’t post when angry or emotional
* Don’t believe or spread fake news without checking sources
* Don’t overshare details about your daily life or location
